Braydon's Speakeasy

Hello! My name is Braydon Orthner. I'm a 19 year-old Canadian. I'm currently attending Bow Valley College for software development. I'm currently living in Calgary, Alberta after recently moving here! My claim to fame is being 6'4, and I'm a jazz fanatic.

I was originally born in Port Sydney, Ontario but my childhood was spent moving from one place to the next. Over the course of my life so far, I've moved 9 separate times across 3 different provinces. starting my life over so many times in such different environments has taught me adaptability above all other things. I can be dropped in any environment and be a completely diferent person to match my surroundings within a month.

I love jazz, whether it be listening or playing it! Playing jazz has taught me the importance of listening to the people around you to avoid getting out of sync and getting tunnel vision. Playing jazz is a team sport, and being able to match the thoughts and feelings of those close to me is one of the most important things to me.
